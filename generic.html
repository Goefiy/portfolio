About Me

Hey! I'm Jade van Schaik, an 18-year-old CMD student from Haarlem. On this website, you can find everything about my work created in my first and second year of the Communication and Multimedia Design program at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences.

In high school, I completed my studies with an Economics & Society profile combined with Computer Science and Social Sciences. Even though I thought I had a slight advantage in the technical subjects of my current program, I find myself better in projects about interaction and design, and practical assignments where I can physically create something.

Besides school, I've been playing hockey for 12 years and I play the bass guitar. I love to take the latter passion to my CMD program. During my Propaedeutics year, I realised that I want to do something with music. Whether it's music production, audio design, sound design, or audio editing. Besides music, am I in love with storytelling and conveying this through moving images, so audio-visual storytelling is also on my radar.

Music runs in my family, everyone participates. Whether it's instruments or vocals. Growing up in the Protestant church, I was exposed to soul and gospel at an early age. This has greatly influenced my music taste today, and what I feel drawn to.

I'm very excited to be able to do something with this in the coming years and discover even more of my own capabilities.
