Lyrics "Why"

The Assignment, The Song, The Result

"In the course Design 2, you will take on a design challenge. A task to which you provide your own creative interpretation. You will create a dynamic design for a song lyric. For this, you will conduct a visual investigation to make substantiated choices."

For this visual assignment, I chose the song "Why" by Dominic Fike. Personally, throughout the assignment, this song reminded me of: the sun, water, grooviness, convertibles, 70s colors, retro, speakers, swimming, and tattoos. Google describes the song as Alternative/Indie. The goal was to visually represent a line from the lyrics in a way that reminds both me and others of the song; it simply has to fit.

I processed the result for these lyrics in Adobe After Effects, with which I already had experience from my free time. I was able to incorporate various elements from my process into the visuals and I am really happy I did. I believe that if I had been given more time, I could have made this video in After Effects even tighter, but for the time we had, I think it turned out well.
